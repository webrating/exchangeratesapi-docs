- **Professional**: 24 hours  
- **Business**: 4 hours
- **Enterprise**: 4 hours + phone support

### When Contacting Support

Include:
- Your email address and plan
- Exact error messages and HTTP codes
- Request ID from X-Request-Id header
- Sample cURL command (with key redacted)

*Check [status page](https://status.exchangeratesapi.com.au) first, then email [support@exchangeratesapi.com.au](mailto:support@exchangeratesapi.com.au)*
