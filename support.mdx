## Business Hours

**Australian Eastern Time (AEST/AEDT)**
- **Monday - Friday**: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Weekends**: Emergency support only for Business/Enterprise plans
- **Public Holidays**: Limited support (Australia and major international holidays)

<Note>
We provide support during Australian business hours as our team is based in Australia and our primary data source (RBA) operates on Australian schedules.
</Note>

## Service Level Agreements

### Uptime Commitments

| Plan | Uptime SLA | Monitoring | Credits |
|------|------------|------------|---------|
| Free | Best effort | Public status page | None |
| Starter | 99.0% | Email alerts | None |
| Professional | 99.9% | SMS + Email | Account credit |
| Business | 99.9% | Phone + SMS + Email | Account credit |
| Enterprise | 99.99%* | Custom alerting | Contract terms |

*Custom SLAs available for Enterprise plans

### Response Time Targets

- Australia/NZ: <50ms average, <100ms p95
- Asia-Pacific: <100ms average, <200ms p95
- Global: <200ms average, <400ms p95

### Maintenance Windows

- Scheduled maintenance: Announced 48 hours in advance
- Emergency maintenance: Immediate notification via status page
- Preferred window: Saturday 2:00-4:00 AM AEST (minimal business impact)
